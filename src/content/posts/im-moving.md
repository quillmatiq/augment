Well, not me, really. This blog is moving.

For those who may not be aware, this blog is currently hosted by [Ghost](https://ghost.org), a popular newsletter tool that I've thoroughly enjoyed using over the past year and change.

That said, my long-term goal has always been to self-host this blog. So, behind the scenes, I've been working on migrating to my own site. I'll talk more about how it's all set up once we're there, but I've added some really cool features, and I think it looks a lot cleaner – I'm excited for you to see it soon!

This change may affect my readers in different ways, so I wanted to make sure you're ready for what's coming. Here's the breakdown:

If you're subscribed via email, you will still be on my email list, but the new emails will be sent from augment@buttondown.email. You'll need to update your filters if you have any, sorry! Also, please keep an eye on your junk mail just in case 📧
If you've subscribed to my RSS feed, you'll continue to get updates ✅
If you've linked to my blog or blog posts in the past, those links will continue to point to a copy of that blog post on the new site 🔄️
If you've subscribed via ActivityPub, that account will unfortunately no longer update; I will, however, have new and exciting ways to follow me on the open social web, so subscribe over email or RSS for when I move over and break down the new site's features 👀
And that's about it! I'm planning on handling the move in the next couple of weeks, and the next post should probably be from the blog's new home. Hopefully, this shouldn't impact most of you in any big way, but if you do run into issues, please feel free to reach out to me at (anuj@augment.ink)[mailto:anuj@augment.ink].

See you on the other side 👋🏼
